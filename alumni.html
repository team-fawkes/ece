ALUMNI

“Our Alumni Is Our Mirror”

Objectives

HSTU Alumni Association plays a vital role by assisting the university in developing linkage with industries, multinational organizations as well as other firms and companies. Besides that, it assists the interns of HSTU in finding internship placement in different organizations. It also provides job opportunities to the students of HSTU.

Preamble

We, the Alumni of the HSTU, develop mutual interest among ourselves, establish mutually beneficial relations between HSTU and its Alumni, and promote the interests of HSTU, do hereby form into an Association and adopt this constitution.
